What is Gen Ai?
->Gen Ai is a Artificial Intelligence that can create  new content     such as text,images,audio,video and code etc.
->it does'nt just analyze data
-> it generates new thing that look like they were created by humans

Traditional Ai = Detect , arrange or organise ,predict.

GenAi = create something new

example : 
Traditional Ai -> identify if the image has a cat or dog
Gen ai -> create a new image of a cat wearing sunglasses.

Gen ai is powered by large model like gpt , gemini trained on huge data.
